## Offensive Tooling

A collection of tools I'm building for penetration testing, malware development research, and CTF work. Everything is designed to run on both **x86_64** and **ARM** architectures.

> Work in progress — tools will be published as they reach a stable state.

---

## In Development

**Cross-Architecture Implant Framework**  
Lightweight C2 implant targeting x86_64 and ARM (including embedded/IoT targets). Focused on minimal footprint and evasion of common EDR signatures.

- Architectures: `x86_64`, `ARM`, `ARM64`
- Language: `C`
- Evasion: AMSI bypass, ETW patching, process injection

---

**AD Enumeration Toolkit**  
Fast enumeration modules for Active Directory environments — wraps common Impacket/BloodHound workflows into a single recon pipeline.

---

**Shellcode Utilities**  
Position-independent shellcode helpers for process injection chains — compatible with both Windows x86_64 and ARM64.
